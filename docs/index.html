Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 09, 2026

Overview

CineFlow Suite is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We believe your data belongs to you. Our desktop application is designed to operate locally on your machine, ensuring that your media files, metadata, and production data never leave your control.

1. Information We Collect

CineFlow Suite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • We do not have user accounts.
  • We do not track your usage behavior (no telemetry).
  • We do not log your activities or the projects you work on.

2. Local Data Processing

All video processing, metadata extraction, and storage occur entirely on your local device. If you use external tools provided within the suite (such as FFmpeg), these tools operate within your system's environment and do not share data with any external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application does not integrate with third-party tracking services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

4. App Store Data

When you purchase CineFlow Suite through the Mac App Store or Microsoft Store, the respective platform handles all financial transactions. We do not receive your credit card information or personal billing details.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions, please contact us via GitHub.
