# CineFlow Suite: Pricing & Versioning Strategy

This document outlines the commercial structure for CineFlow Suite, ensuring transparency for our community of filmmakers and photographers.

## 1. Distribution Model
CineFlow Suite is a **Premium, Paid Application**. 
- **Developer Type:** Individual (Alan Alves)
- **Model:** Licensed via App Store (macOS) and Microsoft Store (Windows).

## 2. Pricing Structure (v1.x)
- **Core License:** One-time purchase.
- **Updates:** All updates within the major version (e.g., 1.1, 1.5, 1.9) are provided at **no additional cost** for existing license holders.
- **Micro-apps:** All current and upcoming micro-apps (Shot List, Match Lab, Review Core, etc.) released during the v1 lifecycle are included in the base license.

## 3. Major Version Upgrades (v2.0+)
When CineFlow Suite transitions to a major new version (v2.0), the following policy applies:
- **Major Upgrades:** v2.0 will be a separate, paid product lifecycle.
- **Early Adopter Discount:** Existing owners of v1.x will be eligible for a **50% Discount** when upgrading to v2.0.
- **Support for Older Versions:** v1.x will continue to function and receive critical security patches for a minimum of 1 year after the release of v2.0.

## 4. Why This Model?
We believe in sustainable software development. By choosing a one-time payment with a discounted major upgrade path (instead of a subscription), we respect the filmmaker's need for stable, owned tools while ensuring we can continue to innovate and support new camera formats (BRAW, RED, etc.) over the long term.
